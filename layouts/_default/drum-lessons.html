Tips and FAQ's

Below is a quick list of tips and FAQ's to consider as you start your journey

Tips

  • Learn the exercise or part slow and build speed later. It's important to teach your muscles how to do something correctly in a relaxed state, and then you can slowly work on speeding up.
  • Always strive to play your exercise or part in a relaxed state. If you can't play it in a relaxed state, it's not ready for prime time.

FAQ List

  • How long will it take me to be good enough to play in my friends' rock band? There is no reason to wait to start a band with your friends. However, mastering the drums takes a lifetime. It's about slow, steady improvement over a period of time.
